Who We Are

The Soul of the Northern Mountains

HNS Travels was born from a deep love for the untamed beauty of Pakistan's northern highlands. We are not just a travel company — we are storytellers, guides, and guardians of one of the most spectacular landscapes on earth.

From the terraced apricot orchards of Hunza to the mirrored stillness of Shangrila Lake in Skardu, every journey we craft is a living poem. We bring you face to face with peaks that touch the sky, cultures older than time, and hospitality that stays with you long after the mountains fade from view.

Khunjerab National Park

Perched at 4,693 metres, Khunjerab Pass marks the world's highest paved international border. A vast, dramatic highland sanctuary home to snow leopards, Marco Polo sheep, and soul-stirring panoramas where Pakistan meets China.
