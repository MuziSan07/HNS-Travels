The Founding Circle

Ali Hassan Mir
Co-Founder & CEO
Born in Karimabad, Ali has spent over 20 years guiding expeditions across Karakoram. He founded HNS Travels with a vision to share Pakistan's north with the world.
Bashir Ahmed Khan
Co-Founder & Head of Operations
A Skardu native and veteran trekker, Bashir oversees all logistics and safety protocols. His meticulous planning has made thousands of journeys seamless and unforgettable.
